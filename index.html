Breast Cancer Detection

Breast cancer is a major health issue, and early detection is crucial for effective treatment. We propose a MATLAB-based image processing method to identify breast cancer by analyzing mammography images for anomalies. Our approach includes pre-processing to enhance image quality and reduce noise. Features such as texture, shape, and edges are extracted and used to train a deep learning model. This model helps differentiate between benign and malignant cases. The method's effectiveness was evaluated using a large dataset of mammography images.

Diabetic Retinopathy Detection

The abstract outlines how machine learning (ML) is improving the detection and imagery of diabetic retinopathy (DR). ML techniques enhance retinal images by reducing noise and improving contrast, helping clinicians better diagnose DR. Convolutional neural networks (CNNs) are highlighted for their ability to automatically detect DR and classify its stages using large datasets. Additional ML techniques like ensemble methods and transfer learning help make models more adaptable across different patient groups and imaging types. The integration of multiple imaging methods, such as OCT and fundus photography, boosts detection accuracy. The abstract emphasizes that ML advancements are key to early DR diagnosis, timely treatment, and reducing vision loss. Future research should focus on refining ML approaches and integrating them into clinical practice.
